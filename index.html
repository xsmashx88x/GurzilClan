PVE Clan & Community Seeking Loyal Active Gamers

Gurzil is a dynamic and engaging PVE clan and community. We prioritize quality over quantity and are committed to fostering a supportive environment for all members. Whether you're a new player or an experienced guardian, you'll find a home with us.

PROTOCOL_01: SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

+

To maintain entry into the main frame, all potential and active members must fulfill the following protocols:

  • Age Limit: Must be 18+ years of age.
  • Discord Registry: Join and remain consistently active within our secure Discord server architecture.
  • Community Engagement: Connect meaningfully with fellow members. You are not a statistic to us—you are a family member the moment you step inside.
  • Destiny Integration: Maintain an active presence in-game.
  • Tactical Experience: Possess basic experience inside Raids and Dungeon environments.
  • Event Accountability: Confirm and show up to events you have committed to attend.
  • General Etiquette: Always maintain respect, kindness, and decency toward your fellow guardians.

PROTOCOL_02: TARGET GUARDIAN PARAMETERS

+

What we look for in our active nodes:

  • Combat Readiness: Maintain decent Light levels on at least one primary character.
  • System Dedication: We seek devoted guardians dedicated to growth alongside the clan.
  • Core Optimization: An excellent sense of humor to keep communication channels lively and warm.

PROTOCOL_03: ACTIVE SECTORS / OPERATIONS

+

Operational pipelines we actively clear together:

  • Seasonal Subroutines: Comprehensive seasonal story content and matchmade activities.
  • Grandmaster Nightfalls: High-tier vanguard operations for optimized endgame gear.
  • Dungeons: Fireteam strikes targeting hidden encounters, lore archives, and weapons.
  • Raids: Complex network coordination requiring communication and high team coordination.
  • And Others... Any additional content the community decides to conquer.
